Choose Your Gate System

Two distinct automation systems tailored to your property layout — both powered by the Walgi smart controller.

Sliding

Walgi Slide Pro

Ideal for driveways with limited swing clearance. The panel glides smoothly on a precision track system with anti-collision technology.

  • Track width up to 8 metres
  • 500 kg load capacity
  • Soft-start / soft-stop motor
  • Manual release in power cut
  • Smartphone & remote control
  • RFID & keypad entry options
Swing

Walgi Swing Elite

A classic dual-leaf design that adds grandeur to any entrance. Hydraulic or electro-mechanical actuators for whisper-quiet operation.

  • Single or dual leaf configurations
  • Leaf length up to 4 metres each
  • Hydraulic & electromechanical options
  • Obstacle detection sensors
  • Smartphone & remote control
  • Video intercom integration
Smart Features

Built for Security & Convenience

Every Walgi gate ships with an intelligent controller packed with features that keep your property safe and connected.

01 / 06

Advanced Security

128-bit encrypted communication, tamper alerts, and anti-lift protection ensure your gate cannot be bypassed by any external attempt.

02 / 06

Mobile App Control

Open, close, and monitor your gate from anywhere in the world via the Walgi app — available on iOS and Android with instant notifications.

03 / 06

GPS Auto-Open

Geo-fencing technology detects when your phone approaches — the gate opens automatically and silently the moment you arrive home.

04 / 06

Solar Ready

Optional solar panel integration keeps your gate powered even during extended outages — complete zero grid dependency for uninterrupted access.

05 / 06

Activity Log

Full timestamped history of every open and close event — accessible via the app or web dashboard at any time, from anywhere.

06 / 06

Multi-User Access

Grant time-limited or permanent access to family, staff, or visitors — each with their own individual PIN codes or unique app credentials.
